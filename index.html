ANTS

silently, we're building

ANTS is a group of heterogenous people ranging from data-scientists, hackers and makers to artists. They all share one belief: small actions can change the world if they are numerous and coordinated.

Small actions:

  • ANTS aint no startup or company.
  • ANTS will never be funded by VC
  • ANTS doesn't belongs to anybody
  • ANTS can't be sold
  • ANTS can survive its founders

Numerous and well coordinated:

  • ANTS is all open-source from core projects to salaries
  • ANTS can be forked anywhere, by anybody

Change the world:

  • ANTS is engaged:
    • we work only on open-data
    • we leverage technology to solve real life problems
    • we don't produce consumer goods
    • ...
  • Is concerned with following themes:
    • environment
    • politics
    • teaching
    • data-journalism

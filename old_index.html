Waste is valuable

It is about making people realize the potent value of their waste and helping them find a solution to make the most of it.

Our solution is

Get data

We have designed sensors capable of
  • estimating affluence based on smartphones wi-fi signatures while respecting privacy
  • knowing the availability of bins in recycling centers

Inform people

Our application answers these questions:
  • how can my waste be useful ?
  • what is the most efficient way ?
  • where and when should i go ?

Organize reuse

We aim at enhancing the reuse by connecting waste producers and waste consumers, whoever they may be.
